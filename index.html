About

Jannis Reiher is a 29-year-old professional drummer and with over 350 shows across Europe, performing in and recording with more than 15 bands and projects. Currently part of the crossover metal/rock band Brunhilde, finishing up a 30-show tour. He has dedicated his life to music, bringing high passion, energy and presence to every stage — from intimate club shows to massive festivals, arenas and three month tours. His style is defined by a powerful, punchy sound fused with technical precision, grounded in a deep commitment to the music and his craft.

"Since I was 16, I knew I would never be doing anything else. Ever since, every decision I made in life has been made for the music and drums. I am grateful to live for and from the music. It's how I live, express, connect, and create."

Why Foo Fighters?

I have endless passion, dedication, creativity, energy, and love to give — and I’m highly eager to bring all of it into your music, vision, and art.

What moves me to reach out isn’t just the legacy. It’s the way Foo Fighters have always been about heart, presence, and putting everything into the music. That’s how I live and perform: all in, eyes closed, in the moment. I’ve shed tears on stage because it feels so raw and true — and, yeah, sometimes because there’s makeup in my eyes.

I know how ambitious this is. But I also know I have the sound, energy and drive to bring something real to the band. I know I was made for this.
